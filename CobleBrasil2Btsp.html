Our Menu

We have three different menus to offer for Lunch, Afternoon, and Dinner services.

Lunch Menu

Our lunch menu features street food classics from Brasil.

Pão de Queijo
Brazilian Cheese Bread - Our most popular item by far!
Coxinha
Battered and Fried Chicken Croquettes
Bolinhos de Bacalhau
Fried Cod Cakes - A fried mix of potatoes and salted cod.
Kibe
A Brazilian twist on a Middle Eastern staple - Fried minced beef patties made with herbs and spice
Picanha
A Gaúcho's Favorite! - A special cut of sirloin skewered and barbecued on our churrasqueira.
Açai na Tigela
Brazilian Açai Bowl served either Northern or Southern style:
  • Northern: Açai smoothie with shrimp and tapioca - the original!
  • Southern: Açai smoothie mixed with guaraná syrup and topped with banana
Mandioca Frito
Cassava Chips
