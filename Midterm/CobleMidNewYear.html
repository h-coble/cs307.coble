Our New Year's Plans

For our New Year's Eve weekend trip, we are offering something somewhat local and familiar but unique and exciting at the same time: a weekend in Nashville!

Food for Thought... For Food

  • Get some authentic Nashville Hot Chicken at Prince's Hot Chicken
  • Enjoy Nashville's Craft Breweries: Yazoo Brewing Company and Diskin Cider
  • Have a taste of Music City's talent at the famous Bluebird Cafe
  • Savor some Southern classics at the Loveless Cafe

Things to Do

  • Visit the Parthenon: A tribute to the Greek temple and its architecture serving as Nashville's art museum
  • Experience Broadway Street's nightlife with live music and more
  • See the history of Music City at the Country Music Hall of Fame
  • Ring in the new year at Nashville's New Year's Eve Live
