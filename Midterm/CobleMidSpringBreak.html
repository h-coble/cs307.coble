The Spring Fever Reliever

Our featured trip for Spring Break is Seattle. Seattle is a one-of-a-kind city known for its seafood markets, hazy weather, logging industry, grunge music, coffee, and of course, the Space Needle. Many musical acts hail from here: Jimi Hendrix, Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Kenny G, Macklemore, Chris Cornell, Ray Charles, Sir Mix-a-lot, Reggie Watts - the list goes on and on. Below is a table laying out the higlights of each day if you follow our itinerary; we've layed out these main events such that they are grouped together either by location or to form a route for other sights to be seen. Remember, it's your trip, so if there's any changes you'd like to make you may do so! We have other notable places listed below as well.
