The Spring Fever Reliever

Our featured trip for Spring Break is Seattle. We believe that Seattle is a one-of-a-kind city. Seattle is known for its seafood markets, hazy weather, logging industry, grunge music, coffee, and of course, the Space Needle. Below is a table laying out the higlights of each day; we've layed out these main events such that they are grouped together either by location or to form a route for other sights to be seen.
